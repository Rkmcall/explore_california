Taste of California

Ojai Olive Company 3 days | $350

Immerse yourself in the culture and lifestyle of working California agriculture. Spend 3 days in your private guest villa at the Ojai Inn located at the base of the fantastic Ojai Olive Company. You'll spend your days wandering the lush olive groves, touring the presses and bottling processes, and assisting the staff in making America's highest grade Extra Virgin Olive oil. Enjoy Tuscan-style meals served Al Fresco overlooking the gorgeous countryside. Day trips include dinner in Ventura, hiking in the Los Padres National Forest, and shopping & lunch in downtown Santa Barbara.

Tour Highlights

  • Olive press & olive grove tours
  • Daily tapas tastings
  • Country breakfast
  • Private lodging at the Ojai Inn & Spa
  • Special dinner in Ventura
  • Day tour of Santa Barbara
  • Hiking in Los Padres National Forest
